“I kept always two books in my pocket, one to read, one to write in.”

~ Robert Louis Stevenson

BookTalk organizes conversations with authors and readers.

We hope to inspire new readers to read books that challenge them to think outside the box. And challenge the old readers to think beyond the printed text and read between the lines.

Our Goal

We read books to read ourselves well. We aim to connect with readers and writers worldwide to become betters readers of books and hearts.

Who We Are

We are a group of readers. Many of us are new readers, while some are seasoned veterans. And, some of us are in the process of becoming readers. If you care about reading, you are most welcome to join us.

Get Involved

You can share with us books you've recently read and loved. Or, share your insights and realizations about books you have once read. Share a few lines with us that left an indelible impression on you. Tell us how you felt a book read you well in a few lines or through a short video.
